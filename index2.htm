KTDash

 
KTDash is a web-based application for running your KillTeam 2021 games.
  • Use the Dashboard to play your games and track operative wounds, Turning Points, Command Points, and Victory Points
  • Build your teams or import a pre-built team
  • Generate names for your operatives
  • Browse the Compendium
