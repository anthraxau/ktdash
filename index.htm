KTDash - Home

KTDash is a web-based application for running your KillTeam 2021 games.

  • Use the Dashboard to play your games and track operative wounds, Turning Points, Command Points, and Victory Points
  • Build your teams
  • Import a pre-built team
  • Generate names for your operatives
  • Browse the Compendium
The site is a "PWA" ("Progressive Web App"), meaning it can be installed on your mobile phone (Android or iPhone). Use your browser menu's "Install" action to install it on your phone.

For developers, the Compendium is available as a JSON dataset here: GitHub
